WinEPTS Configurazione

  • Copiare la cartella WEClient_test in C:\
  • Eseguire C:\WEClient_test\WEClientSettings.exe

- Listening Port = 60001
- Trace Level = 5


- Tab Servers:

- Listening Port = 30000

- Tab WinEPTS:
